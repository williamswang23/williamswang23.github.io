BIS:Trade sentiment and the stock market: new evidence based on big data textual analysis of Chinese media

Trade sentiment and the stock market: new evidence based on big data textual analysis of Chinese media

notes:

  1. collecting media text data,which include internet media and traditional media reports data, to analyse market sentiment, the author make a conclusion about market return after the media reported some pessimistic or optimistic news.
  2. based on CNN to discriminate text sentiment, the author distributes news to three categories, +1, 0, -1.
  3. text analysis has two distinct ways, which are word frequency and tone of text analysis. in author’s view, the latter is more reasonable because of former would confuse real means of news in most cases.
  4. the author construct several indicators, TCI, TSI. then, building a linear model to distribute different independent variable’s contribution.
  5. the linear model is:

  6. Second, we show that a sentiment index that represents the positive or negative tone (TSI) explains better equity market variability than an index that simply counts the frequency of specific keywords (TCI). Overall the TSI contributes 10% of the model capacity to explain equity price variability, while the TCI contributes only 5%.

  7. Third, we show that TSI using social media (web, forum, WeChat) contributes about 9% of the explained model variability, while a TSI based on traditional media only contributes 1%.

views

  1. linear function is a best approximate indicator when you just want to make some simple conclusion or preliminary analysis, for non-discriminate problem.
  2. the quality of CNN is crucial factor for outcome of this paper. considering important influence of order of sentence’s word, there is not suitable for text analysis.
  3. the machine learning models are appropriate for making categories to market sentiment analysis.
  4. the trade relationship between different countries determine different responsible of them, but, we can also get explicit relationship of them from posteriori data.
  5. TCI is more sensible than TSI,that means TCI including more noise.
