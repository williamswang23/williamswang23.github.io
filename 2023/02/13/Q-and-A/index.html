How to ask?

Albert Einstein once said, “ The mere formulation of a problem is far more essential than its solution.” So before asking ChatGPT any questions about financial investment, no matter how much you understand NLP, first ask yourself if the question you’re asking falls within the scope of what an NLP model can solve.

Albert Einstein: The mere formulation of a problem is far more essential than its solution, which may be merely a matter of mathematical or experimental skills. To raise new questions, new possibilities, to regard old problems from a new angle requires creative imagination and marks real advances in science.
